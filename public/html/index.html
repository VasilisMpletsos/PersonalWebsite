Projects

The i-Stevva is an device specially designed for low power consumption, to monitor your fileds. It can provide the user with live data like humidity, if rains, soil PH, temprature and also send messages when considered necessary. We aim to make an online platform and process data by region, so with the aid of AI to give helpful imformations and tips!

We are currently also developing ChargeSpot, a building where passengers at the beach of Thessaloniki will be able to charge their smartphones, electric wheelchairs, electric scooters and many more. It is based entirely on reneable energy like solar panel and wind enrgy generation. It will be constructed on recycled materials.

Car Telemetry is an online representation of the dynamic behaviour of FSAE vehicle. It shows steering wheel angle data, suspension loads, tempratures from many parts of the vehicle like engine and coolers and finally it shows RPM.

Mask Radio was an online radio station that i created on the 2020 Carantine. Users can send their songs with searched directly on the site from youtube, while the can add some dedications. Their suggestions are added to the database and the broadcaster can choose and play some of them. Also users can chat on the site and have fun all together!
